## Overview


The Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory conducts research in particle physics, focusing on the study of fundamental particles and forces. They operate powerful particle accelerators to advance our understanding of the universe, exploring topics such as dark matter, dark energy, and the properties of subatomic particles. The laboratory is a hub for scientists to collaborate and push the boundaries of human knowledge in these areas.
