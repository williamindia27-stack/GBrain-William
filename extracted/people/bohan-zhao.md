## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Bohan Zhao is a researcher at Tsinghua University specializing in foundation models for financial time series analysis. He is a key contributor to Kronos, a foundation model engineered specifically for financial K-line data that employs specialized tokenization and autoregressive pre-training on over 12 billion K-line records across 45 global markets and 7 temporal granularities. His work demonstrates substantial performance improvements, including 93% gains in price forecasting RankIC over existing time series foundation models and 22% improvements in synthetic K-line generation fidelity. Zhao's research addresses critical limitations in applying general-purpose time series models to financial domains, showing that domain-specific pre-training frameworks can significantly enhance accuracy and generalization across diverse quantitative finance tasks. His contributions position foundation models as essential tools for developing robust, scalable solutions to end-to-end financial time series analysis in both research and institutional settings.
