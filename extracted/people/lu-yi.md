## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Lu Yi is a researcher at Alibaba Group specializing in large language model-based agent systems and agentic application frameworks. He contributes to the development of AgentScope, a developer-centric framework that abstracts foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules to enable flexible composition of agentic applications. His work integrates the ReAct paradigm to combine explicit reasoning with tool-based actions, enabling agents to analyze tasks and iteratively refine their steps in closed-loop interactions. The framework addresses critical challenges in agent-environment interaction by supporting parallel tool calls, asynchronous executions, and real-time steering for industrial-grade performance. Through AgentScope's built-in agents and comprehensive toolkits, Lu Yi's research aims to provide a practical foundation for building scalable, adaptive systems that can solve complex real-world problems and support diverse interactions across academic research and industrial practice.
