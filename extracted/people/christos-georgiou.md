## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Christos Georgiou is a cosmological researcher based at The Barcelona Institute of Science and Technology who specializes in addressing methodological challenges in large-scale cosmological analyses. He is a core contributor to Smokescreen, a Python package designed to mitigate experimenter bias through data-vector blinding and encryption methods that preserve statistical properties while concealing the true cosmological signal. His work leverages Firecrown likelihoods and the SACC data format to implement a reproducible, five-step blinding procedure that ensures theoretical models remain consistent between concealment and inference phases. Georgiou's contributions emphasize the critical importance of scientific integrity in cosmological measurements, particularly in contexts where traditional double-blind experimental designs are technically infeasible. Through his collaborative efforts on tools like Smokescreen, he advances the field's capacity for transparent, verifiable analysis practices that strengthen confidence in cosmological parameter estimation across the wider observational astronomy community.
