## Overview


<!-- bio-agent -->
Zeyu Hu is a researcher at LIGHTSPEED focused on advancing the evaluation and development of vision-language model agents in interactive environments. He contributes to the OmniGameArena project, a unified benchmark that assesses VLM game agents across twelve Unreal Engine 5 games spanning Solo, PvP, and Coop gameplay modes, moving beyond limitations of single-agent evaluations. His work incorporates the Improvement Dynamics Curve harness, which measures how VLM agents adapt and refine their performance through autonomous reflection across multiple interaction rounds. The research demonstrates that heterogeneous agents including commercial VLMs, open-weight models, and specialized game policies exhibit divergent improvement patterns, with peak performance often emerging mid-curve rather than at convergence. Through this comprehensive benchmarking framework, Hu's work enables more realistic assessment of VLM agent capabilities in complex, multi-agent scenarios with direct implications for developing more robust and adaptive AI systems in real-world applications.
