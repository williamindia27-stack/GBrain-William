## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Zongliang Fu is a researcher at Tsinghua University specializing in machine learning applications to financial time series analysis. He co-develops Kronos, a foundation model engineered specifically for financial K-line data that employs specialized tokenization and autoregressive pre-training across over 12 billion records from 45 global markets and 7 temporal granularities. His work demonstrates substantial performance improvements in price forecasting (93% RankIC boost over leading baseline models), volatility prediction (9% MAE reduction), and synthetic K-line generation (22% improvement in fidelity). Fu's research addresses critical limitations in existing Time Series Foundation Models by creating a unified, scalable framework capable of generalizing across diverse financial markets and quantitative finance tasks. His contributions enable more robust and reliable financial analysis tools for both academic research and institutional applications in global financial markets.
