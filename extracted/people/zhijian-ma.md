## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Zhijian Ma is a computer scientist at Alibaba Group specializing in large language models and agentic applications. He contributes to the design and development of AgentScope, a developer-centric framework that abstracts foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules into unified, extensible interfaces for building agent-environment interactions. His work on AgentScope demonstrates expertise in implementing practical agentic systems that support parallel tool calls, asynchronous execution, and real-time steering capabilities, alongside integrated built-in agents such as browser-use, deep research, and meta-planner agents. The framework he helps develop incorporates a comprehensive suite of toolkits, including evaluation modules and Studio interfaces, to streamline workflows and enhance developer experiences in deploying agents at scale. Ma's research vision focuses on providing scalable, adaptive, and effective foundations for agentic applications that solve complex real-world problems through flexible interactions with users and diverse environments.
