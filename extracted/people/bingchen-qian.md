## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Bingchen Qian is a computer scientist at Alibaba Group specializing in the development of large language model-based agentic systems and developer-centric frameworks. He contributes to AgentScope, a comprehensive framework that abstracts foundational components for building agentic applications, including message, model, memory, and tool modules with unified interfaces and extensible architecture. His work implements the ReAct paradigm within AgentScope, enabling agents to perform explicit reasoning and iterative task refinement through closed-loop tool use and execution observation. The framework he helps develop supports industrial-grade performance features including parallel tool calls, asynchronous executions, and real-time steering, while integrating built-in agents such as browser-use, deep research, and meta-planner agents alongside comprehensive evaluation and development toolkits. His research addresses the critical challenge of scaling flexible and efficient agent-environment interactions for practical real-world applications. Through AgentScope, Qian's work provides a foundational platform for deploying safe, adaptive, and effective agentic applications across both academic and industrial contexts.
