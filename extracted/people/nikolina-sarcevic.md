## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Nikolina Šarčević is a cosmological researcher who specializes in addressing methodological challenges in large-scale astronomical surveys and data analysis. She contributes to efforts to ensure scientific integrity in cosmological experiments through work on data-vector blinding and encryption techniques designed to mitigate experimenter bias. As a collaborator on the Smokescreen project, she participates in developing standardized implementations of blinding methodologies that work with Firecrown likelihoods and the SACC data format to prevent analysts from unconsciously tuning their pipelines toward expected results. Her work emphasizes the importance of transparency and reproducibility in cosmological analyses, making concealment procedures verifiable by the broader scientific community before and after data unblinding. Through these contributions, Šarčević advances best practices for maintaining the integrity of measurements in modern cosmological experiments where designing double-blind or single-blind studies presents significant technical challenges.
