## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Yi Yu is a computer scientist at Alibaba Group whose research focuses on the design and development of frameworks for large language model-based agentic applications. As a core contributor to AgentScope, Yu participates in the creation of a developer-centric framework that abstracts foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules to enable flexible composition and extensibility. The framework Yu helped develop implements the ReAct paradigm to combine explicit reasoning with tool-based actions, allowing agents to analyze tasks iteratively and refine their approaches in closed-loop interactions with their environments. AgentScope provides industrial-grade performance through support for parallel tool calls, asynchronous executions, and real-time steering capabilities, alongside integrated agents such as browser-use, deep research, and meta-planner implementations. Yu's work addresses the critical challenge of building scalable and adaptive agentic applications that can solve complex real-world problems while maintaining safe execution through runtime sandboxes and practical deployment pathways.
