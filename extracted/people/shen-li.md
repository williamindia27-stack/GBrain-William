## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Shen Li is a researcher at Alibaba Group working in the domain of large language models and agentic applications. He contributes to the development of AgentScope, a developer-centric framework designed to empower agents to combine intrinsic knowledge with dynamic tool use for addressing real-world tasks. His work focuses on abstracting foundational components—message, model, memory, and tool modules—into unified interfaces that enable flexible composition for practical applications. Through AgentScope, Li and his team have implemented industrial-grade performance features including parallel tool calls, asynchronous executions, and real-time steering capabilities, alongside built-in agents such as browser-use, deep research, and meta-planner agents. His research vision centers on building scalable, adaptive, and effective agentic systems that can solve complex problems through flexible interactions with both users and environments.
