## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Pedro H. Costa Ribeiro is a cosmologist affiliated with Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro who specializes in addressing methodological challenges in large-scale cosmological surveys. He contributed to the development of Smokescreen, a Python package that implements data-vector blinding and encryption methods to mitigate experimenter bias in cosmological analyses. His work focuses on ensuring the scientific integrity of measurements by applying cosmology-dependent shifts to observed data vectors while preserving their statistical properties, utilizing Firecrown likelihoods and the SACC format for standardized implementation. Costa Ribeiro's contributions emphasize the importance of transparency and reproducibility in analysis pipelines, enabling collaboration-wide adoption of blinding methodologies that can be inspected and verified by the broader research community. His research reflects a commitment to developing practical computational tools that safeguard against unconscious biases in parameter inference and theoretical modeling within observational cosmology.
