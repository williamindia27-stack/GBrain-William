## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Mehul Damani is a researcher at MIT working in reinforcement learning and language model optimization. He contributes to advancing test-time search capabilities through Vector Policy Optimization (VPO), a novel training approach that combines multi-answer generation with stochastic reward scalarizations to enable language models to produce diverse solutions spanning the Pareto frontier. His work demonstrates that VPO-trained models substantially outperform standard baselines across multiple domains including multi-hop question answering, logic reasoning, navigation, tool use, and coding tasks, while unlocking problems unsolvable by existing methods within constrained search budgets. The Vector Policy Optimization framework addresses the critical challenge that standard post-training methods typically result in low-entropy response distributions that hinder effective exploration during inference. His research contributes to a broader vision of improving language model performance in complex tasks requiring both exploration and exploitation through principled diversity-aware training methods.
