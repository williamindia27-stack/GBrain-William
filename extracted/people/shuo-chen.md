## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Shuo Chen is a researcher at Tsinghua University specializing in machine learning applications to financial time series analysis. He is a core contributor to Kronos, a foundation model designed specifically for financial K-line data that addresses critical limitations in existing Time Series Foundation Models through specialized tokenization and autoregressive pre-training on over 12 billion records from 45 global markets. His work demonstrates substantial empirical advances, achieving 93% improvement in price forecasting accuracy and 9% reduction in volatility prediction error compared to leading baselines. Chen's research integrates deep learning with quantitative finance, enabling robust generalization across multiple markets and temporal granularities while supporting diverse applications from price prediction to synthetic financial data generation. His contributions highlight the potential of domain-specific foundation models to transform financial data analysis and support decision-making in financial institutions.
