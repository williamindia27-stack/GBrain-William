## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Dawei Gao is a computer science researcher at Alibaba Group specializing in large language model systems and agentic applications. He leads the development of AgentScope, a developer-centric framework designed to empower autonomous agents by combining intrinsic knowledge with dynamic tool use for real-world problem-solving. His work abstracts foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules with unified, extensible interfaces, while implementing the ReAct paradigm to enable iterative reasoning and action loops. Gao's framework demonstrates industrial-grade performance through support for parallel tool calls, asynchronous execution, and real-time steering, alongside integrated agents such as browser-use, deep research, and meta-planner variants. His research vision centers on creating scalable and adaptive agentic applications that bridge academic innovation and practical deployment, providing comprehensive tooling including evaluation modules and development studios to streamline the entire workflow from conception to production.
