## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Haohao Luo is a computer scientist at Alibaba Group working on the design and development of large language model-based agentic applications. He is a core contributor to AgentScope, a developer-centric framework that abstracts foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules to enable flexible construction of agentic systems. His work on AgentScope emphasizes practical engineering solutions for building scalable and efficient agent-environment interactions, incorporating the ReAct paradigm to combine explicit reasoning with tool-based actions in closed-loop execution cycles. The framework he helped develop integrates industrial-grade capabilities such as parallel tool calls, asynchronous execution, and real-time steering, along with built-in agents for specialized tasks like browser automation and deep research. Through this work, Luo contributes to bridging academic research and industrial practice by providing a comprehensive foundation that enables both rapid prototyping and production deployment of complex agentic applications.
