## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Weirui Kuang is a computer science researcher at Alibaba Group specializing in large language models and agentic applications. He is a core contributor to AgentScope, a developer-centric framework that abstracts foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules to enable flexible and efficient agent-environment interactions. His work implements the ReAct paradigm within AgentScope, combining explicit reasoning with tool-based actions to enable agents to iteratively refine their problem-solving strategies. The framework he helped develop supports industrial-grade performance through parallel tool calls, asynchronous executions, and real-time steering capabilities, while providing built-in agents such as browser-use, deep research, and meta-planner agents for practical deployment. Kuang's research demonstrates how modular, extensible design principles can bridge the gap between academic research on language model agents and production-ready systems capable of addressing complex real-world problems.
