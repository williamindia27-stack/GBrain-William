## Overview


<!-- bio-agent -->
Yiyu Wang is a researcher at LIGHTSPEED specializing in vision-language model evaluation and game-based benchmarking for artificial intelligence agents. He contributes to the development of comprehensive evaluation frameworks for VLM agents, including the OmniGameArena benchmark that spans twelve Unreal Engine 5 games across Solo, PvP, and Coop gameplay modes. Wang plays a key role in designing the Improvement Dynamics Curve harness, a novel methodology that measures how VLM agents adapt and refine their performance through autonomous reflection across multiple rounds of repeated interaction. His work demonstrates that commercial and open-weight VLMs exhibit measurable improvement potential when subjected to iterative skill prompt refinement, though performance gains do not uniformly correspond to linear progress. Wang's research contributes to more realistic and comprehensive assessment of AI agents in complex, interactive environments, advancing the understanding of how language models can generalize across diverse game scenarios and learn from experience.
