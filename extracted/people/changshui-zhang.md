## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Changshui Zhang is a researcher at Tsinghua University specializing in machine learning applications to financial time series analysis. He leads work on foundation models for quantitative finance, most notably co-authoring Kronos, a specialized foundation model designed to handle financial K-line data through innovative tokenization and autoregressive pre-training across over 12 billion records from 45 global markets. His research demonstrates substantial performance improvements in critical financial tasks, achieving 93% gains in price forecasting accuracy and 22% improvements in synthetic data generation fidelity compared to existing time series foundation models. Zhang's work addresses fundamental limitations in applying general-purpose foundation models to financial domains, establishing unified frameworks that generalize across diverse markets and temporal granularities. His research vision centers on enabling more robust and reliable models for end-to-end financial analysis that can serve both academic researchers and financial institutions.
