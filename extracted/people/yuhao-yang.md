## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Yuhao Yang is a researcher at the University of Hong Kong specializing in the evaluation and deployment of autonomous agents in financial markets. He co-developed the AI-Trader benchmark, a novel evaluation platform that assesses Large Language Models in real-time financial decision-making across multiple markets including U.S. stocks, A-shares, and cryptocurrencies. His work employs a fully autonomous minimal information paradigm where agents must independently search, verify, and synthesize live market information without human intervention. Through systematic evaluation of six mainstream LLMs, Yang's research reveals critical limitations in current autonomous agents' risk management capabilities, cross-market generalization, and execution performance under actual market volatility. His research addresses fundamental gaps in how autonomous financial agents are evaluated, moving beyond static benchmarks to realistic, live assessment environments that expose the gap between agent capabilities and practical financial application requirements.
