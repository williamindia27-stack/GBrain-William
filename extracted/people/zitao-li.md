## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Zitao Li is a computer scientist at Alibaba Group specializing in large language models and agentic application development. He is a core contributor to AgentScope, a developer-centric framework that abstracts foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules to enable flexible agent-environment interactions. His work on AgentScope demonstrates expertise in modular design and extensibility, implementing key features such as parallel tool calls, asynchronous executions, and real-time steering capabilities that deliver industrial-grade performance for LLM-based applications. Through the framework's integration of the ReAct paradigm and built-in agents like browser-use and deep research agents, Li contributes to making complex reasoning and tool-based interaction accessible to practitioners. His research vision centers on providing practical foundations for building scalable and adaptive agentic systems that can address real-world problems while maintaining safety through runtime sandboxing and rapid deployment capabilities.
