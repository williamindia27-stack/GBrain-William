He contributes to the development of critical infrastructure for modern cosmological surveys, including work on data-vector blinding techniques designed to eliminate experimenter bias from observational analyses. His work on Smokescreen demonstrates a commitment to implementing sophisticated encryption and blinding methodologies that preserve the statistical properties of cosmological datasets while preventing unconscious tuning toward expected results. Through his involvement in collaborative cosmological projects, Vitenti has helped establish reproducible and transparent procedures for handling potential bias in complex analyses, ensuring that concealment procedures can be inspected and verified by the broader scientific community.
