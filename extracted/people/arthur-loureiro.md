## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Arthur Loureiro is a cosmologist at Stockholm University whose research focuses on addressing systematic biases and ensuring scientific integrity in large-scale cosmological analyses. He is the lead developer of Smokescreen, a Python package that implements data-vector blinding and encryption methods to prevent experimenter bias by applying cosmology-dependent shifts to observed data while preserving statistical properties. His work leverages Firecrown likelihoods and the SACC data format to create standardized, transparent, and reproducible blinding procedures that can be integrated into collaboration-wide analysis pipelines. Loureiro's technical contributions demonstrate high standards for code quality, achieving 94% code coverage and providing comprehensive test suites that verify the physical correctness of blinding computations across any cosmological parameters available in pyCCL. His research vision emphasizes making concealment procedures transparent and inspectable by the wider scientific community, thereby strengthening the reliability and trustworthiness of cosmological measurements that inform our understanding of the universe.
