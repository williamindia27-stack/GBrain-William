## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Liuyi Yao is a researcher at Alibaba Group working in the domain of large language models and agentic applications. He contributes to the design and development of AgentScope, a developer-centric framework that abstracts foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules for building flexible agentic applications. His work emphasizes the ReAct paradigm, which enables agents to combine explicit reasoning with tool-based actions in iterative closed loops to address complex real-world tasks. He is instrumental in implementing AgentScope's practical features including parallel tool calls, asynchronous executions, built-in agents such as browser-use and deep research agents, and a comprehensive evaluation and deployment infrastructure. His research vision centers on creating scalable, adaptive, and effective agentic systems that bridge intrinsic knowledge with dynamic tool use, positioning such frameworks as essential infrastructure for both academic research and industrial practice.
