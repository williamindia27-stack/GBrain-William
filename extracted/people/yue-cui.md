## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Yue Cui is a computer scientist at Alibaba Group specializing in large language models and agentic applications. He contributes to the development of AgentScope, a developer-centric framework designed to empower agents by combining intrinsic knowledge with dynamic tool use for real-world problem-solving. His work on AgentScope focuses on abstracting foundational components including message, model, memory, and tool modules with unified interfaces, while implementing the ReAct paradigm to enable agents to reason and act iteratively in closed loops. Cui's contributions extend to advancing practical infrastructure for agentic systems, including support for parallel tool calls, asynchronous executions, real-time steering, and runtime sandboxing to ensure safe production deployment. His research vision centers on creating scalable, adaptive, and effective frameworks that facilitate flexible agent-environment interactions and support diverse LLM integrations across both academic and industrial applications.
