## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Jian Li is a researcher at Tsinghua University specializing in financial machine learning and time series foundation models. He contributed to the development of Kronos, a foundation model specifically designed for financial K-line data that addresses critical limitations in existing Time Series Foundation Models through a novel two-phase framework combining specialized tokenization and autoregressive pre-training. Kronos demonstrates substantial empirical improvements across multiple financial tasks, including 93% improvement in price forecasting RankIC over leading alternatives and 9% lower MAE in volatility prediction. The model leverages pre-training on over 12 billion K-line records across 45 global markets and 7 temporal granularities, enabling robust generalization across diverse financial markets and tasks. His work represents a significant advancement in applying foundation model techniques to quantitative finance, providing financial institutions and researchers with a versatile tool for more accurate and reliable financial time series analysis.
