## Overview



<!-- bio-agent -->
Wei Xu is a researcher in machine learning and quantitative finance at Tsinghua University. He contributes to the development of foundation models for financial time series analysis, including work on Kronos, a specialized foundation model designed to handle financial K-line data through novel tokenization and autoregressive pre-training approaches. His research addresses critical limitations in existing Time Series Foundation Models by leveraging massive heterogeneous datasets spanning over 45 global markets and 7 temporal granularities. The methods he helps develop achieve significant empirical improvements, including 93% gains in price forecasting performance and enhanced capabilities in volatility prediction and synthetic financial data generation. His work demonstrates that unified, scalable pre-training frameworks can provide robust and generalizable tools for end-to-end financial time series analysis across diverse markets and quantitative finance applications.
